Day-by-Day Plan

Follow the checkboxes as you go.
MonWelcome, routines & mini Express demo
  1. Bellringer (2–3 sentences): One brand you love and why.
  2. Icebreaker: On an index card, doodle a quick personal logo (symbol/initials/icon). Share if called.
  3. Open: Canvas → Week 1 Module. Skim the four assignments.
  4. Watch: 8-minute demo in Adobe Express (title, three short text blocks, one icon, two fonts, 2–3 colors).
  5. Exit ticket: One thing you’re excited about + one question (Canvas text entry or sticky note).
TueCareers & Draft Infographic
  1. Bellringer: Which job sounds cooler (event planner, social media manager, or sports marketer)? Why?
  2. Mini-lesson: Career snapshots (tasks, tools, skills, growth).
  3. Create draft in Express: Title (career) · 3 Daily Tasks · 3 Tools · 2 Skills · 1 Growth Path · 1 icon · footer “Created by [Name] — Marketing”. Export PNG.
  4. Submit Draft PNG: Upload to Canvas → Career Infographic — Draft.
WedWorkshop, Peer Review, Final Infographic
  1. Bellringer: What visual thing (color/font/style) makes an ad memorable?
  2. Tune-up checklist: Palette (2–3), clear type pairing, margins/alignment, hierarchy (most important info pops first).
  3. Refine & export: Lock colors/type; export near-final PNG by ~70 min.
  4. Peer Review ×2: Each partner leaves 1 strength + 1 suggestion using stems.
  5. Submit Final: Upload PNG + 2–3 sentence caption to Career Infographic — Final.
FriPromotional Mix & Discussion Post
  1. Bellringer: A product you use daily — best way a company could reach you?
  2. Mini-lesson: Advertising · PR · Sales Promo · Personal Selling · Digital/Social.
  3. Group task: For 3 products, pick 2 effective channels + 1 weak channel. Give two reasons tied to audience/message/cost.
  4. Discussion Post: 3–4 sentences — 2 best channels + why; 1 weak channel + why. Reply to a peer by Sun 8pm.
  5. Turn in bellringers: Submit Bellringer Journal — Week 1 in Canvas.
